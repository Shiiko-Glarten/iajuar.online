Discography

As a solo artist, I have been producing music under different monikers for 8 years, now releasing exclusively from the current project: iajuar. My background is as a pianist, but I also enjoy playing drums and guitar more casually.

Recently, I had the honor and pleasure of composing for the enormously talented Boolean Gyre Ensemble - releasing a set of 14 pieces titled Oasi Taas from performances recorded at Hawkes Chamber Hall in Minneapolis. I remember this project fondly as an opportunity for which I cannot thank the entire ensemble thoroughly enough.

My musical curiosity is continually renewed in the electronic realm as I try to exhaust the capabilities of LogicProX, incorporating analog synthesizers purchased from others as well as those made by myself along with digital instruments like drum machines and soft synths. I find the ability to digitally manipulate, glitch, chop, and otherwise mangle sound endlessly satisfying as a new frontier of musical possibility, one from which I never tire and appreciate immensely. My favorite instrument is my laptop, closely followed by an upright grand piano which I will always love.
