About Life Music Synthesizers Leather

My name is Pearson Schwartz For the past 5 years I’ve worked at a record store in Minneapolis while producing music, designing analog synthesizers, and hand making leather goods in my spare time. In 2025, I sold what I owned and have been prioritizing travel and a nomadic lifestyle in order to create more under my project: iajuar.

(“EE-ah-zhwar”) → /ˈiɑˌʒwɑr/

* i → “ee” as in see

* a → “ah” as in father

* j → “zh” sound as in measure

* u → “w” glide as in quick

* ar → “ahr” as in far
